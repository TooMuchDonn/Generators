For power level, please enter either a single number or a range between two numbers, such as 8-10

It can be helpful to remember that the Gamemaster's Guide says to Choose a power level for your villain based on the character’s role: within 1 level of the series PL for a lieutenant, 1–5 levels higher for a master villain, and 3–5 levels lower for a henchman. So for a PL10 series, aim for PL11-15 for a master villain, PL9-11 for a lieutenant, and PL 5–7 for a henchman.
