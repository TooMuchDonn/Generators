Character Generator

This is a legacy generator that I had originally put together. It's intended to generate characters of any type, without names or descriptions. It gives a collection of attributes and some habits and potential flaws.

M&M 3e Villain Generator

This generator creates a M&M 3e character. This is intended to be used to generate enemy NPCs for M&M encounters, but could generate friendly NPCs if you ignore fields directly related to being a villain. The generation, however, is weighted towards creating characters with evil alignments. To generate a more random alignment, you can roll a d9 or use my Alignment Generator.
